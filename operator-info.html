Amiya

Amiya is a 5★ Core Caster who specializes in DPS and is given as one of the starting Operators and (for the purpose of giving out Amiya's Token.png Amiya's Token) one of the first clear rewards of 2-10, 3-8, 4-9, 6-17, and 7-18; she is never available from headhunting and recruitment otherwise. As a Core Caster, Amiya's attacks deal Arts damage to one target at a time with a moderate ATK and attack interval, and a range of 3×3 tiles with a 1-tile extension upfront at Elite 1, and has a decent DP cost. Unlike most other Operators, Amiya's Potential cannot be improved with Epic Caster Token, making her potential tied to the player's progress in the Main Theme.
